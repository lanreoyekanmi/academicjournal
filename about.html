About Us

The Academic repository is decidated to bringing together all the available journal within the univeristy of Ilorin and giving easy access to the user. If you have any questions or comments, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Aims.

  • To improve the quality and aid the development of academic publishing at the Univeristy of Ilorin in accordance with international standards.
  • To enhance the visibility and usage of national academic journals worldwide.
  • To ensure the implementation of the Unilorin Journal Management System efficiently.

Goals of the Repository.

  • Passionate about making research projects easily accessible to all student and staff of the univeristy. With this in mind, I hereby, developed this Institutional Repository as my final year project to serve as a platform for sharing undergraduate projects.
  • I believe that education is the key to unlocking one's full potential, so I've committed to developing innovative solutions that can enhance the learning experience of students.
  • My goal is to provide a user-friendly platform where students can easily access and review Institutional journals output by the University of Ilorin. I believe that by sharing knowledge and experience, we can create a community of lifelong learners who are committed to academic excellence.
  • I've committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism in all our dealings with our stakeholders. My team is constantly working to improve the platform and provide exceptional service to the users.
